A little bit about me

Learn a little bit more about me.

Hi my name is Torben but I am mostly known as kipteam. I am a Belgian high school student with 17 years of experience at being alive.
I began coding at a young age starting out with scratch. Later on I would move my focus to Minecraft development, making my own game Modifications and Plugins. During my Minecraft era I learned about server management and had my first community projects popping up. I have ran and managed several Minecraft related projects for large and small communities. I ran several big Community projects with entire custom games and features and high player counts. I also made my first few bucks around this time. My last Minecraft project required a website which was my queue to get enrolled in a whole new world. At first I started making simple and small websites but quickly I would start making more advanced and complicated projects.

As a non-programmer I love to spend time with my friends, go ice skating, play the piano and draw web related notes or designs in my notebooks.
