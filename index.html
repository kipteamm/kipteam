A little bit about me

Learn a little bit more about me.

Hi my name is Torben but I am mostly known as kipteam. I am a Belgian high school student with a proud 17 years of life experience.
I began coding at a young age starting out with scratch. Later on I would move my focus to Minecraft development, coding Mods and Plugins. Also learning server management and getting my first community projects up and running. I have ran and managed several small Minecraft related projects for Friends. But Later started focussing on big Community projects with custom created games and features. Making my first few bucks in the process. My last Minecraft project required a website which was my queue to get enrolled in a whole new world. At first I started making simple and small websites. Quickly I would start making more advanced and complicated projects.

As a non-programmer I love to spend time with my friends, draw web related notes or designs in my notebooks, actually draw (mostly pixel art) and chat on Discord.
